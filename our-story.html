Our Story

Editors For a Better World is a non-profit organisation built around video editing and design resources. We are run by a small team of volunteers/socially conscious video editors aiming to contribute our help towards social issues, and empower you with the means to do the same.

Using donations from all corners of the video-editing community, we raise money targeting global issues and organizations.

Thanks to the support shown for our cause, in the past we have donated over $230 to charities such as the Palestine Children’s Relief Fund, The Trevor Project, and Afghanistan Crisis Children’s Relief.
